Hello! I'm a Software Engineer

I'm a software engineer graduate from MIT ADT University, Pune, with a specialization in Intelligent Systems. My journey in the tech world has been both diverse and enriching, marked by hands-on experience and a passion for continuous learning.

I started my professional career at Altair, where I worked as a Software Intern in the Hypermesh Core Development Team. During my time at Altair, I was responsible for managing Hypermesh software code, addressing bug fixes, and implementing Requests for Enhancements (RFEs). This role honed my problem-solving skills and gave me a deep dive into software development processes.

Following my internship, I ventured into the realm of full-stack development at E9ine. Over the course of a year, I contributed to various projects, including County Mall and RepsUk. These projects allowed me to apply my skills in Node.js, Vue.js, React.js, JavaScript, Python, MongoDB, Express.js, and cloud technologies, resulting in robust and efficient applications.

My technical expertise is complemented by a solid foundation in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) using C++ and C. Additionally, I am an enthusiastic learner of iOS development, constantly exploring new ways to enhance my skill set and stay abreast of the latest industry trends.

I am passionate about leveraging technology to create impactful solutions and am always eager to collaborate on innovative projects. Let's build the future, one line of code at a time!
